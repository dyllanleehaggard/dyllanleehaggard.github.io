// 01 — About

The analyst behind the numbers.

"My goal is to automate and accomplish as much as possible — build workflows that make hard things easy, and use data and code to put every team on the front foot."

I wear three hats and all of them fully. As a Data Engineer, I build and optimize the pipelines that make data usable at scale. As a Data Architect, I design systems and schemas that hold up six months later. As a Data Analyst, I translate all of it into decisions that move things. These aren't different jobs — they're the same job done end to end.

By day I'm on the Credit Analytics team at Stifel Bank & Trust — building CECL infrastructure, converting cascading views into materialized staging tables, and automating reports that used to take hours. By night and weekends I'm the Video/Data Analyst for the St. Louis Ambush — building their analytics stack from scratch, coding film in Hudl SportsCode, and generating game sheets that reshape how the team defends in the 2nd half.

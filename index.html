I'm a data analyst who lives at the intersection of sports intelligence and financial analytics — two worlds that share the same core challenge: making confident decisions under uncertainty.

By day, I work on the Credit Analytics team at Stifel Bank & Trust, building the models and infrastructure that drive loan risk decisions. By night (and weekends), I'm the Video/Data Analyst for the St. Louis Ambush in the MASL, crafting tactical game sheets, opponent breakdowns, and coaching intelligence dashboards that help the team compete at the highest level of indoor soccer.

I also co-founded Footy Collective, a soccer analytics consulting firm where I collaborate with a team of researchers to publish deep-dive reports on the game's most pressing questions — from competitive balance to the long-term commercial impact of the 2026 World Cup.

My goal is simple: build tools and tell stories with data that actually change how decisions get made.
